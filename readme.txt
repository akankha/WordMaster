== Description ==
Elevate your content creation game with WordMaster: Word Count & Reading Time Pro, the ultimate tool for WordPress authors, bloggers, and content creators. Seamlessly integrated into your WordPress editor, this versatile plugin offers two invaluable features that enhance your content and engage your readers.

= Key Features =
- **Word Count:** Effortlessly track the exact word count of your articles and blog posts. Whether you're crafting concise blogs or comprehensive essays, WordMaster ensures you hit your writing goals with precision.

- **Reading Time Estimation:** Delight your readers by providing estimated reading times for your content. This feature empowers your audience to plan their reading sessions and dive into your articles with confidence, knowing how much time they'll need.

- **Customization Options:** Tailor the appearance of your word count and reading time display to match your website's aesthetics. Choose from a range of styles, fonts, and positions, ensuring seamless integration with your site's design.

- **User-Friendly Interface:** WordMaster boasts an intuitive interface, ensuring users of all skill levels can efficiently access and utilize its features. Its seamless integration into the WordPress editor streamlines word counting and reading time estimation.

- **Performance Optimization:** This lightweight and efficient plugin won't compromise your website's performance. WordMaster is designed to provide a smooth user experience for both content creators and readers alike.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `word-master` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the plugin settings under the 'WordMaster Settings' menu in your WordPress admin panel.
